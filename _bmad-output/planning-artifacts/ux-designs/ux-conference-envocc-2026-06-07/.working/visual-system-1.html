Conference Room Booking

The adopted design system for the ENVOCC internal room-booking tool. Earthy greens for an environmental & occupational-health identity, a warm copper accent for personal touchpoints, and a calm cream canvas. Noto Serif Thai carries headings and figures; Noto Sans Thai carries every interface surface.
